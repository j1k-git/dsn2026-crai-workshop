## Overview
CRAI 2026 focuses on making agentic AI systems dependable and secure through a **systems-oriented, compositional approach**:
decomposing agentic workflows into analyzable modules with explicit interfaces, fault boundaries, and verifiable properties.

---

## Topics of Interest
**A. Compositional Architectures & Software Engineering**
- Modular agentic workflows; verified orchestration; sandboxing & isolation; software metrics

**B. Resilience, Fault Tolerance & Consensus**
- BFT for agents; redundancy patterns; self-healing; failsafe defaults & graceful degradation

**C. Formal Verification & Runtime Assurance**
- Compositional verification; runtime monitoring; neurosymbolic verification; model-based V&V

**D. Security, Privacy & Ethics**
- Secure agent communication; privacy-preserving tool interaction; adversarial defenses; auditability

**E. Domain-Specific Resilient Agents**
- CPS agents (power/medical/manufacturing), autonomous driving, healthcare & industrial automation
